Reference guide for ratings
Rating scale
5
Top Performer. Has significantly exceeded expectations and is performing well above their current position.
4
Exceeds Expectations. Has performed above average and stretched themself to achieve positive feedback and ratings.
3
Meets Expectations. Has performed on par with their colleagues, and as per expectations for their role / position.
2
Needs Improvement. Has performed below average. Focus should be placed on identifying measures to improve performance.
1
Significantly Underperforms. Has significantly underperformed in this period — a performance improvement plan should be implemented as soon as possible.
Please provide specific supporting instances or examples wherever you rate a team member a 4 or 5 on any of the criteria below.
Parameters & assessment areas
01Chargeability 40%
  • If it is below 70%, Manager to provide comments to support the reasoning.
  • We expect a staff and senior to have a chargeability of minimum 80%.
  • Managers or immediate seniors to ensure the same.
  • Ensure internal meetings, admin, and training time are within reasonable limits or negligible.
  • Notification to the partner or manager if non-chargeable time is expected to be high.
  • If the member is under / over occupied, they have informed manager early / timely.
02Accountability5%
  • Takes ownership of their deliverables; admits mistakes.
03Commitment5%
  • Shows dedication to the firm and a passion to succeed.
  • Takes initiative and looks for ways to contribute beyond their core duties.
  • Identifies and solves problems without being prompted.
  • Doesn't need constant supervision or micromanagement.
  • Actively seeks feedback and development opportunities.
  • Open to change and adapts well.
  • Shows interest in growth within the organization rather than short-term gains.
04Business & process development5%
  • Proactively contributes ideas and engages in techstorming sessions.
  • Identifies new opportunities to improve processes and support business growth.
05Quality10%
  • Delivers high-quality services, aligning with our top priority.
  • Emphasises quality delivery, allowing the recipient to focus less on correcting basic issues.
  • Enables managers to concentrate on critical and key areas by ensuring the rest of the work is reliable.
  • Adheres to established standards and working paper formats, maintaining consistency and professionalism.
06Timeliness & punctuality7.5%
  • Consistently ensures punctuality in completing tasks and delivering outputs within manager-assigned timelines.
  • Demonstrates responsibility by planning leaves in advance, seeking approval beforehand, and communicating proactively to avoid disruption to the team.
  • Maintains and updates timesheets regularly and in a timely manner.
07Communication10%
  • Effective communication is crucial and involves active engagement with the entire team, timely updates regarding planned leaves, and transparent communication of any challenges or issues affecting the delivery of tasks.
  • Maintains respectful and professional interactions with both seniors and juniors, fostering a collaborative and supportive work environment.
  • Engages in effective and timely communication with the UK team.
08Participation5%
  • Demonstrates willingness to participate in all team events, trainings etc.
09Work ethics & discipline7.5%
  • Demonstrates skills like professionalism and time management — filling timesheets in a genuine manner and ensuring honesty, integrity towards work.
  • Mindful of accurately reporting hours worked. Respects the system and does not just try to "fill the time sheets".
  • Instead of just clocking hours, aims to be productive and values outcomes over optics.
  • Understands that inflated timesheets can impact billing, client trust, or internal cost assessments.
Note: to be assessed in conjunction with the other parameters.
10Team player5%
  • Demonstrates strong teamwork by collaborating effectively with colleagues, maintaining a positive attitude, and contributing to collective goals.
  • Understands the value of team development and consistently offers support to enhance group success.
  • Enhances group success through shared effort and collaborates effectively with colleagues.
